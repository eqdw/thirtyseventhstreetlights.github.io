37th Street Lights

The weirdest block in Austin.


directions_walk

Visit

The lights mostly occur between Guadalupe and Home St on 37th. Entering 519 W 37th St, Austin, TX 78705 or La Tazza Fresca into your GPS will get you here. The best house though is actually on the corner of 35th Street and Cedar just around the corner at the end of the block.

The best way to experience the lights is to walk it as the street becomes backed up with cars and many of the coolest things can only be seen by walking off the street a bit. Especially the Stuffed Animal Petting Zoo which is a must see.

To the weird and beyond.

About

The Lights are a tradition started in the late eighties on the street.

Though they happen around Christmas time, these aren't Christmas lights.
Donate

We are an all volunteer group of neighbors that put on the lights. Your donations help us put it on and keep it going. About half the block are renters and we/they especially appreciate the donations as many are students and funds are tight.

We prefer donations of equipment, especially:

  • LED Christmas Lights
  • Extension Cords
  • Weird sculptures

    • We do accept small paypal donations, though we are not a 501(c)3 so we cannot offer any form of tax breaks.
